Understanding Wildfires in Southern California

Southern California faces some of the most aggressive wildfire seasons in the U.S. due to dry brush, strong winds, and extreme heat. These fires spread rapidly, threatening lives, homes, and infrastructure. Having the right emergency tools can make the difference between panic and preparedness.
